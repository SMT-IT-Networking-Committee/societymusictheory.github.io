This year's Restaurant Guide focuses mainly on restaurants near the hotel, along or just off Burrard Street and Davie Street. Some exceptions are listed to include restaurants that offer 100% vegetarian/vegan, halal, or BC First Nations (aboriginal) menus, and winners of this year's Vancouver Restaurant Awards. Please take note of the Guide's wayfinding tips, especially the distinction between West End--downtown--and West Side (south of downtown over the Burrard, Granville, or Cambie Bridges).

The Craft Beer Guide includes a suggested walking tour of local craft breweries in the Mount Pleasant area, a short cab ride away on the other side of False Creek (see link to map below).

Activities In and Around Vancouver

Short trips from the conference hotel

  • Davie Street (close restaurants and shops)
  • Walk to the Seawall and beaches
  • Stanley Park (magnificent forests, an 8.8km Seawall with stunning views, and various kid-friendly attractions; bike rentals available nearby)
  • Granville Island (market, boat rentals, artisan shops, Arts Club theatre, Theatresports)
  • Kitsilano
  • Chinatown
  • Gastown

Museums

  • Bill Reid Gallery of Northwest Coast Art (downtown)
  • Museum of Anthropology (on the UBC campus)
  • Museum of Vancouver (just across the Burrard Bridge)
  • Vancouver Art Gallery (downtown)

Kid-friendly activities

  • Telus World of Science (on the False Creek waterfront)
  • Vancouver Aquarium (in Stanley Park)

Some other fun Vancouver neighborhoods to visit

  • Mt. Pleasant
  • Commercial Drive
  • Hastings-Sunrise (recently rebranded as “East Village Vancouver”)

North Vancouver and beyond

  • Seabus trip to Lonsdale Quay Market
  • Grouse Mountain (gondola, wildlife demonstrations, hikes)
  • Lynn Canyon Park (canyon/forest hikes and a suspension bridge)
  • Lynn Headwaters Park (more hikes)
  • Capilano Suspension Bridge
  • Deep Cove
  • Sea-to-Sky Highway toward Squamish and Whistler (views of Howe Sound, waterfront picnic areas at Porteau Cove, Sea-to-Sky Gondola [open through Nov. 6], more hikes)
