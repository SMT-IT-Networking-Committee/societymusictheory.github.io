Reframing Music Theory

Elizabeth Marvin (Eastman School of Music), Moderator

{% include paper_titles.html %}

Abstracts

The field of music theory has been shaped by intersecting systems of knowledge and power that involve race, ethnicity, gender, sexuality, ability/disability, culture, and nationality. These four papers critique the confining frames within which our field has been operating and explore ways in which to reframe what constitutes music theory.
