Here is a list of information you might find useful while at the conference. You should also visit our local arrangements guide for more activities in the San Antonio area.

  • The Children’s Corner is located by Bonham E, and is open on Thursday, 2:00-5:00; Friday and Saturday, 10:00-5:00; and Sunday, 10:00-12:00
  • There is a mothers’ nursing room is located in Suite 656
  • Unisex bathrooms are located on the 2nd & 4th floors
  • Guests of the hotel may text 210.405.6209 for concierge needs
  • The Hyatt will provide bills under the door the last night of your stay. You may skip the checkout line and check out using the TV if you wish. Just leave the key in the room.
  • The city of San Antonio offers many electic scooters for rent, in case you wish to scoot about to locations nearby.
