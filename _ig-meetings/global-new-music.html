Join us for a series of short presentations and listenings on global musical modernisms, including works by composers from Japan, Singapore, South Korea, China, and Estonia.

Panelists

  • "Tomoko Deguchi (Winthrop University). "Toru Takemitsu, Far Calls, Coming, Far! (1980)"
  • Anton Vishio (William Paterson University). "Jo Kondo, Paregmenon (2011)"
  • Barry Wiener (NYC). "Akemi Naito, The Woman in the Dunes for solo percussionist (2012)"
  • Jungmin Lee (Montclair State University). "Unsuk Chin, Cello Concerto (2008)"
  • Gavin Lee (Soochow University, China). "John Sharpley, Emptiness (2002)"
  • Amy Baur (UC Irvine). "Helena Tulve, L'Équinoxe de l'âme (The Equinox of the Soul) (2008)”
  • Ya-Hui Cheng (University of South Florida). "Cui Jian, A Piece of Red Cloth (1991)”
