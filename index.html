Welcome to the 2020 SMT virtual conference! The menu above includes a search page, a list of interest group meetings, and information about the online conference.

How does the conference work?

Presenters in our various sessions have created materials (videos, slides, papers, handouts, etc) that are available on this website. You can find these materials by browsing to a session and viewing a paper. These materials are password protected and available to conference registrants only.

Each session and meeting will have synchronous/verbal discussions as well as asynchronous/text-based discussions. Synchronous discussions on Zoom occur at the date and time listed on the schedule, and they can be accessed by navigating to the session page and clicking the button. Each session and interest group will also have a text-based discussion on Slack. You can access those by navigating to the session page and clicking the button. These discussion platforms are password protected and available to conference registrants only.
