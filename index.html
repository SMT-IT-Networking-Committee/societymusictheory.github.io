Welcome!

How does the conference work?

Presenters in our various sessions have created materials (videos, slides, papers, handouts, etc) that are available on this website. You can find these materials by browsing to a session and viewing a paper.

Sessions and interest group meetings have both synchronous/verbal discussions and asynchronous/text-based discussions.

  • Synchronous discussions occur on Zoom occur at the date and time listed on the schedule, and they can be accessed by navigating to the session page and clicking the button. Find more information here.
  • Each session and interest group will also have a text-based discussion on Slack. You can access those by navigating to the session page and clicking the button. You can find more discussion on the conference Slack here.

Presenter content and discussions are password protected and available only to conference registrants.

How do I find AMS sessions?

AMS sessions are not listed on the schedule here. Joint AMS/SMT sessions are, however; and navigating to those sessions on the schedule will lead you to Pathable, who is hosting the AMS conference.

Other helpful items

  • If you are having difficulty accessing content, lost your password, or if you've misplaced your conference registration email, send an email to support@societymusictheory.org

  • If you'd like to install the conference platform as an app on your phone, follow this guide.
