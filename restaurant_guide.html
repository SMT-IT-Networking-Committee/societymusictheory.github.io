Restaurant Guide

North Market
59 Spruce Street, Columbus, OH 43215

All restaurants in North Market are take-out only, but there are places to sit on the second floor.

  • Satori Ramen Bar (Ramen)
  • Nida’s Sushi and Thai (Asian)
  • Hubert’s Polish Kitchen (Slavic)
  • Flavors of India (Indian)
  • Lan Viet (Vietnamese)
  • Dos Hermanos (Mexican)
  • Firdous Express (Mediterranean)
  • Hot Chicken Takeover (Nashville-style Hot Chicken)
  • Momo Ghar Market (Tibetan dumplings)
  • Hoyo’s Kitchen (Somali)
  • Little Eater (Local Ohio Grown)
  • Market and Boar (Ohio Barbeque)
  • Omega Artisan Baking (Breads and pastries)
  • Pistacia Vera (Macarons, gluten-free cakes)
  • Pastaria (Fresh Pasta)
  • Sarefinos (Pizza)
  • Jeni’s Splendid Ice Creams
  • Stauf’s Coffee (Local coffee roaster)
